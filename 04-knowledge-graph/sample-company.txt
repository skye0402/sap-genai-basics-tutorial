TechVision Inc. is a software company founded in 2018 by Maria Chen and David Park. 
The company is headquartered in San Francisco, California.

Maria Chen serves as the CEO and has a background in artificial intelligence research.
David Park is the CTO and previously worked at Google as a senior engineer.

TechVision develops CloudSync, an enterprise data synchronization platform. 
CloudSync supports real-time data replication across multiple cloud providers including AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud.

The company also offers DataGuard, a security product for data encryption and access control.
DataGuard was launched in 2021 and has been adopted by over 500 enterprise customers.

TechVision has partnerships with major cloud providers. 
In 2022, they announced a strategic partnership with Microsoft to integrate CloudSync with Azure services.

The company employs approximately 200 people and has offices in San Francisco, New York, and London.
